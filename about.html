About El-Rhema Hospital

El-Rhema Hospital was established in January, 2005, with a vision to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to the growing community of Gowon Estate and its surrounding areas in Lagos, Nigeria.

What began as a modest medical facility has grown into a comprehensive healthcare institution serving thousands of patients annually. Our journey over the past two decades has been marked by continuous improvement, expansion of services, and an unwavering commitment to patient care.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional healthcare services through compassionate, personalized care, advanced medical technology, and a team of highly skilled professionals dedicated to improving patient outcomes.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Treating every patient with empathy and understanding
  • Excellence: Maintaining the highest standards of medical practice
  • Integrity: Upholding ethical principles in all our dealings
  • Innovation: Continuously adopting best practices in healthcare
  • Community: Being an active partner in community health and wellness

Our Facilities

Located at plot 441, 41 Road, Opposite Palmville School, American Junction, Gowon Estate, Lagos, Nigeria, our hospital features:

  • Modern inpatient wards with 24/7 nursing care
  • Fully equipped operating theaters
  • Advanced diagnostic imaging center
  • Comprehensive laboratory services
  • Emergency care unit
  • Maternal and child health center
  • Onsite pharmacy

At El-Rhema Hospital, we believe that healthcare is a right, not a privilege. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that every patient receives the care they need in a comfortable, professional environment.
